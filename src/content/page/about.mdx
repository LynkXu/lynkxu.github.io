#### Bio
- Born in Anhui in 1998. Attended college in Qingdao, completed my master's degree in Shanghai, and have been living here ever since.
- Started tinkering with smartphones and flashing custom ROMs in high school, which became my gateway to computer science.
- Graduated in 2023 and currently working as a Backend / Full-stack Developer at a foreign enterprise.

#### Life
- Love sports, including but not limited to weightlifting, running, and cycling.
- Enjoy staying outdoors; looking at the blue sky and white clouds always makes me happy.
- Hanging out with friends is joyful, but I also cherish my alone time.
